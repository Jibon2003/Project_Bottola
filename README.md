# Project_Bottola
Unique Features of the Website
1. Smart Product Search & Filtering
The website includes a search and filtering system that allows users to quickly find products based on categories, price range, seller, and keywords. This improves the shopping experience by reducing the time required to locate desired items.
2. Friday Flash Auction
Every Friday, selected products are featured in a limited time auction where users can place competitive bids. This weekly event increases user engagement, encourages participation, and provides buyers with opportunities to purchase products at attractive prices, simultaneously help sellers to clear old stock.
3. Tuesday Twist
Every Tuesday, the user who accumulates the highest amount of active screen time on the website earns a free spin (twist) on the "Spin & Win" wheel. The wheel offers exciting rewards such as exclusive discounts, promotional coupons, and special offers, encouraging regular platform activity and user retention.
4. Order Tracking
Users can monitor the progress of their orders through a visual progress bar that displays each stage of the purchasing process, including Order Confirmed, Processing, Ready for Pickup, and Completed. This provides transparency and keeps buyers informed throughout the transaction.
5. Points Loyalty Program
Buyers earn reward points for every successful purchase and other eligible activities on the platform. These points can later be redeemed for discounts, promotional offers, or exclusive benefits, encouraging customer loyalty and repeat purchases.

6. Easy Product Listing for Sellers
Registered sellers can easily upload products by providing details such as images, descriptions, pricing, and category information. The streamlined listing process enables sellers to showcase their products efficiently while maintaining consistent product information.
7. Seller Rating & Review System
After completing a transaction, buyers can rate and review sellers based on product quality, communication, and overall service. This transparent feedback system builds trust within the marketplace and helps future buyers make informed purchasing decisions.
8. Integrated Buyer Seller Chat
The platform provides a built-in messaging system that enables buyers and sellers to communicate directly before purchases. This feature allows users to discuss product details, clarify questions, and coordinate order collection without leaving the website.
9. Product Promotion
Sellers can promote their products on the website to increase visibility and attract more potential buyers.
Our Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
Unlike traditional online marketplaces that rely on third-party delivery services, our platform eliminates delivery charges by using the university campus café as the official pickup location. After a purchase is confirmed, buyers and sellers meet at the campus café to exchange the product, making the process simple, secure, and cost effective. This campus based pickup model reduces delivery costs, shortens waiting times, and creates a convenient marketplace exclusively for university students.
